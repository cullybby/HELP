Jewelry can be so much more than just a bit of glitter on the skin. It's a secret message, a marking of time, a token of love, an ode to independence. The pieces we choose, be they studded silver or cascading gold, are a deeply personal expression of self. It only makes sense that we seek out the extraordinary to do so.

 

Greig Porter is best known for his lush displays of color—you will often find the jewelry cases of Santa Fe Dry Goods gleaming like a candy shop thanks to Greig's influence. However, he excels in the neutral realm as well.

 

Our current favorites are on the grey and brown spectrum—chocolate diamonds, imperial topaz, polished tourmaline. Nestled amongst 18 and 22K gold, these stones sing with a vintage demeanor. As the term "neutral" suggests, this is an easy palette to wear—seamlessly melding with most skin types and wardrobe styles.

 
 

In selecting your next piece, we invite you to meditate not only on its beauty—rare is it to find a piece in these collections that does not inspire the imagination—but on its personal potential. It can be such a pleasure to see our identities reflected in stone and gold, even if we are simply looking for a bit of sparkle. Besides... we hold firmly that jewelry does not need a time or place to be celebratory... the simple act of wearing it is a celebration in and of itself.
